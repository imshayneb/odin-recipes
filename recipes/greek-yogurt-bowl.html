Greek Yogurt Bowl Recipe

Description

This is an easy-to-make, delicious greek yogurt bowl that packs in a bunch of flavor and is high in protein. This recipe is quick to make which makes this recipe a winner.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup of greek yogurt
  • 1 cup of blueberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup of granola

Steps

  1. Spoon greek yogurt into a bowl
  2. Toss in your blueberries, banana, and granola
  3. Mix and enjoy
