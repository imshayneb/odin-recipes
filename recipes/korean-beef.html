Korean Beef Recipe

Description

If you've ever had a craving for a tangy, Asian-inspired ground beef dish, this is the one for you! With under 30 minutes of prep time, this recipe is sure to be a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup diced green onions

Steps

  1. Bring a pan to medium heat and add in your raw ground beef
  2. While the meat is cooking, dice up your green onions and set aside
  3. When your beef is brown, add in your brown sugar and sesame oil
  4. Stir and leave on medium low heat for 10 minutes
  5. Serve and enjoy
